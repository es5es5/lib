- Editor > Minimap: Enabled = false
- Terminal › Integrated › Shell: Windows = C:/Program Files/Git/bin/bash.exe
- [Ctl+Shift+LeftArrow] : cursorWordStartLeftSelect
- [Ctl+Shift+rightArrow] : cursorWordStartRightSelect
